# Win Animation Design Concepts

> **Status key:** ✅ Implemented · ❌ Cut · 📋 Planned

When the theme is high-tech and sleek, traditional paper confetti feels too manual.
These animations disrupt the interface to create an undeniable "Win" moment,
from subtle-but-clear to high-energy.

---

## Concept 1: 📋 Code Burst & Data Stream

The most direct adaptation of "confetti" to a cyber-terminal theme — data leaves
the center of the grid.

- **The Effect:** When solved, the grid cells flash brightly. Instantly, tiny
  green hexadecimal strings (`0A B2`, `0xFF`, `0101`) burst outward from the
  center, cascading over the UI like data rain.
- **The Details:** A single, extremely bright neon-green flash washes over the
  entire background for ~100 ms.
- **The Vibe:** *Pure information satisfaction. You didn't just solve it — you
  unlocked the data.*

## Concept 2: 📋 Particle Matrix Implosion

All about energy and physics — a massive focal point right on the puzzle.

- **The Effect:** A powerful energy vortex forms in the centre. Tiny glowing
  green particles are pulled inward from the screen edges, gathering speed.
- **The Details:** As particles collide at the centre they create a bright,
  soft-focused energy sphere that "pops" outward, pushing a circular ripple
  (shockwave) across the background.
- **The Vibe:** *Stabilizing or capturing a powerful anomaly.*

## Concept 3: 📋 Holographic Data Overlay

Instead of moving objects, this event uses a massive change in UI state.

- **The Effect:** The UI glitches briefly and is replaced with a large-scale
  holographic text overlay (e.g. "SOLVED" or "ACCESS GRANTED") composed of
  bright, scanning green lines.
- **The Details:** Faint scan lines slowly sweep across the scene. The
  "Check Answer" button glows with a pulsing hologram texture.
- **The Vibe:** *Advanced computer interface revealing a secure message.*

## Concept 4: 📋 Vector Shockwave & Ring Expander

Dynamic and highly kinetic — burst plus undeniable motion.

- **The Effect:** An expanding circular shockwave of geometric green vector
  lines erupts from the centre. It expands to the screen edge, briefly blurring
  the background, then forms a clean expanding ring that fades, followed by
  smaller ripple rings.
- **The Details:** A very quick diagonal scan-flash over the whole UI. Use
  very thin, bright, geometric lines for the shockwave.
- **The Vibe:** *A release of contained digital pressure.*

## Concept 5: 📋 Matrix Ripple

A reality-warping visual — the screen itself distorts outward from the centre.

- **The Effect:** Concentric rings of distortion pulse outward from the grid
  centre, "bending" the visible content like a stone dropped in water. A
  matrix-green tint washes across each wavefront.
- **The Details:** Multiple staggered wavefronts expand at slightly different
  speeds. Each ring carries a faint glow and warps the grid lines behind it. The
  amplitude decays as rings reach the screen edge, leaving the scene still.
- **The Vibe:** *You cracked the simulation — reality stutters.*

## Concept 6: 📋 Confetti Burst

A classic celebration moment, tuned to the cyber-terminal palette.

- **The Effect:** Rectangular confetti pieces explode outward from the grid
  centre, tumbling and spinning with realistic physics (gravity, air drag,
  flutter rotation).
- **The Details:** Pieces are neon-coloured rectangles (cyan, green, magenta,
  yellow) that start at high velocity and decelerate under gravity. Each piece
  has independent 3D rotation (X, Y, Z axes) so the tumble looks natural. A
  thin bright flash precedes the burst.
- **The Vibe:** *Pure celebration — no subtlety, just joy.*
